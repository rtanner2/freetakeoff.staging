Rough Ridge Trail

Moderate • 1.4 miles • Milepost 302.8

Spectacular 360-degree views from rocky outcrops. One of the most photographed spots on the Parkway.

Craggy Pinnacle Trail

Easy • 1.4 miles • Milepost 364.4

Short hike to stunning 360-degree views. Famous for rhododendron blooms in June.

Easy Flowers

Waterrock Knob Trail

Moderate • 1.2 miles • Milepost 451.2

High-elevation trail with panoramic mountain views. One of the highest points accessible by car.

Linville Falls Trail

Multiple viewpoints of the spectacular 45-foot waterfall. Various trail options for all abilities.

Essential Information

Best Times to Visit

  • Spring: Wildflowers, waterfalls (March-May)
  • Summer: Full access, rhododendrons (June-August)
  • Fall: Peak foliage (September-October)
  • Winter: Limited access, icy conditions

What to Bring

  • Sturdy hiking boots
  • Weather layers (temps vary by elevation)
  • Water and snacks
  • Map or GPS device
  • First aid kit

Safety Tips

  • Check weather and road conditions
  • Tell someone your hiking plans
  • Stay on marked trails
  • Be aware of elevation changes
  • Carry emergency supplies

Regional Highlights

  • Virginia: Shenandoah Valley views
  • North Carolina: Highest peaks, waterfalls
  • Tennessee: Smoky Mountain gateway
  • Georgia: Southern terminus
